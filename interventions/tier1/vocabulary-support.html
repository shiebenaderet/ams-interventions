What It Is

Systematic teaching and reinforcement of academic vocabulary essential for content understanding through cards, word walls, and games.

What Quality Implementation Looks Like

‚úÖ In the Classroom (What TO Do):

  • Academic vocabulary identified in advance (before the unit)
  • Vocabulary taught explicitly with multiple exposures (not just "look it up")
  • Students use vocabulary in speaking and writing, not just memorize definitions
  • Visual supports available (word wall, anchor charts)
  • Low-pressure practice opportunities (games, partner talk)

‚ùå Red Flags (What NOT to Do):

  • Vocabulary list given, never referenced again
  • Students told to "look up definitions" with no teaching
  • Word wall is decorative only (never used)
  • Only assess vocabulary through quiz/test
  • Academic language used by teacher but not explicitly taught

Implementation Steps

Before the Unit

  • Identify 5-10 essential vocabulary words (not 30!)
  • Choose which words get deep instruction vs. quick definition
  • Plan multiple exposures across the unit

Explicit Teaching (10-15 min per word)

  • Say the word (phonetically complex words: spell it, break it into syllables)
  • Student-friendly definition (not dictionary definition)
  • Show visual/example: "Here's what this looks like in our content..."
  • Check understanding: Turn and tell your partner what _____ means
  • Use in context: Model 2-3 sentences using the word
  • Student practice: Write your own sentence, share with partner

Ongoing Use

  • Day 1: Explicit teaching
  • Day 2-3: Use word in lesson, have students use it
  • Day 4: Vocabulary game or review activity
  • Throughout: Require word use in student work ("Use at least 3 vocabulary words in your paragraph")

Word Wall Routine

  • Add words as you teach them (not all on day 1)
  • Reference during lessons: "Look at our word wall - which word describes this?"
  • Weekly word wall scavenger hunt: Find the word that means...
  • Students use word wall during assignments (this is expected, not cheating)
